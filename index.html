NUMATS COLOR MIXER

INSTRUCTIONS

With this colour mixer you can design your own NUMAT TPV mix.

  1. Select your colour granules from the swatches below. You can blend as many as you like.
  2. Use the + buttons to add granules and the - buttons to remove them.
  3. When you have finished your design and if you like the mix, save or print it as a PDF.

Click reset to start again.
